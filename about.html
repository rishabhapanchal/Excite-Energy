About Excite Energy

Designing and installing high-yielding clean energy systems across California since 2014.

Our History & Mission

Excite Energy was founded in 2014 as "Solar Norcal" before expanding services and rebranding as a complete clean energy and electrical engineering contracting firm.

With over 1,000 successful projects completed across the Sacramento Valley and Bay Area, we have built a reputation based on absolute transparency, engineering precision, and robust support. Unlike marketing-heavy solar companies that rely on high-pressure sales reps and subcontracted crews, we lead with technical expertise. We operate as a full-service C-10 electrical contractor and licensed engineering firm, keeping all design and installation operations completely in-house.

This structural approach guarantees that your residential or commercial solar and battery storage system is built to code, runs safely, and achieves the exact electricity savings predicted by our math models.
