Why Go Solar in California Today?

According to the Solar Energy Industries Association (SEIA), California is ranked #1 in the nation for solar capacity.

Solar equipment and installation prices have fallen 43% over the last 10 years, while utility rates from non-renewable energy sources continue to spike. While state rebates and net metering policies (like NEM 3.0) have shifted, combining solar with smart battery storage still represents one of the highest returning investments a homeowner can make in California.

Excite Energy provides custom residential solar design, engineering, and installation across Sacramento, San Jose, El Dorado Hills, and the surrounding regions. We make sure your home's system is fully compliant, grid-optimized, and aesthetically pristine.
