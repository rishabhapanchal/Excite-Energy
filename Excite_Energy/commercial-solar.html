Protect Your Bottom Line

Commercial energy costs represent a significant operational expense that can be mitigated through custom-engineered solar and battery storage solutions.

With utility demand charges making up a large portion of commercial bills, Excite Energy designs systems targeting peak load shaving to lower monthly costs. Whether your goal is to meet state environmental requirements, satisfy Title 24 mandates, or optimize corporate sustainability metrics, we provide commercial-grade engineering stamp plans and complete interconnect management.

Excite Energy's in-house engineering team manages every detail of the process: structural engineering audits, utility interconnect agreements, Division of the State Architect (DSA) coordination for schools, and post-installation operations monitoring.
