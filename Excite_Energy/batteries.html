Battery Storage Systems

Maximize your solar efficiency and protect your property from grid blackouts with advanced energy storage.

The Next Frontier in Clean Energy

Battery storage technology is advancing rapidly. The cost of lithium-ion cells has decreased substantially over the last decade due to global supply chain innovations.

With utility companies moving to complex Time-of-Use (TOU) rates, electricity is most expensive during the late afternoon and evening hours (4 PM - 9 PM). A battery system allows you to store the free solar energy produced by your panels during the day and discharge it during high-rate peak hours. Under California's Net Billing Tariff (NEM 3.0), adding battery storage is essential to capturing the full value of a solar installation.

Excite Energy works with the world's leading battery cell and power electronics manufacturers, including Panasonic, Tesla, LG Chem, Samsung, BYD, and CATL, to ensure your system features cell longevity, safe thermal management, and smart utility rate tracking.

Residential Applications

Home Energy Resilience

Protect your household against utility blackouts, public safety power shutoffs (PSPS), and winter storm grid failures. Our residential storage configurations dynamically power key circuits:

  • Critical Loads: Refrigerator, lights, medical gear, routers.
  • Load Shifting: Run your house on battery during expensive 4pm-9pm hours.
  • Seamless Transfer: Switch to backup power in milliseconds during outages.
  • Brands: Tesla Powerwall 3, Panasonic EverVolt 3.0, FranklinWH.
Commercial Applications

Demand Charge Mitigation

Businesses face complex demand charges where peaking power consumption for just 15 minutes can trigger high monthly utility charges. Commercial batteries solve this via intelligent load shaving:

  • Peak Shaving: Discharge batteries to suppress demand spikes.
  • Title 24 Compliance: Fulfill California Energy Commission mandates.
  • Decentralized Storage: Use modular, panel-mounted battery systems (e.g. Yotta) to avoid bulky containers, fire suppression rooms, and concrete pads.
  • Brands: Yotta Energy, SolarEdge Commercial, SMA, Tesla Megapack.
